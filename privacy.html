Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

NexusBot collects minimal data required for its functionality. This include:

  • User IDs: To track economy balances, levels, and warnings.
  • Guild IDs: To store server-specific settings and configurations.
  • Message Content: Only when commands are invoked or for moderation purposes (e.g., purge command). We do not store message history permanently.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely for:

  • Providing bot features (economy, leveling, etc.).
  • Server moderation and security.
  • Improving bot performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored securely in local JSON files. We retain your data only for as long as you are active in servers using NexusBot. If you delete your account or the bot is removed, your economy and level data may be archived for up to 30 days before permanent deletion.

4. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard practices to protect your information. Access to the raw data files is restricted to the bot developers. We never ask for your passwords or Discord credentials.

5. Third-Party Services

NexusBot operates on the Discord platform. By using our bot, you are also subject to the Discord Privacy Policy. We do not use any other third-party analytics or trackers.

6. Cookie Policy

This static dashboard does not use cookies and does not track your browsing behavior outside of this site.

7. Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data or its immediate deletion by joining our support server or contacting the bot developers.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the development team.
