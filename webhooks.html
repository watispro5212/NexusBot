What are Webhooks?

Webhooks are a way for apps to send automated messages or data to a specific Discord channel. NexusBot uses authorized webhooks to provide real-time logging, moderation alerts, and automated economy updates.

When you authorize NexusBot, you grant it the ability to create and manage these secure connections to keep your server running smoothly.
